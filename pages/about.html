Acharya provides a unique social and cultural experience at its annual festival that is filled with talent showcases, music, cultural collaborations, and a lively atmosphere to enrich the students’ college life. Acharya Habba attracts numerous students from various colleges to participate in various inter-collegiate competitions, events and showcases.

The enormous campus comes alive with the music, mascot, and much more! The three-day annual college festival hosts more than 50 events across various departments. “Habba” in its true essence is a grand festival and a showcase of the best Acharya has to offer.

Acharya Habba- launched in 2005, and has now emerged as one of the biggest and best college festivals in Bangalore, with every year the festival getting bigger and better. Acharya’s enormous, picturesque campus is an added advantage to hosting thousands of students and visitors. Some of the best performers and the biggest names in the music industry have marked their presence in Acharya Habba.

Acharya Habba grew from a few thousand attendees in its first year to 30,000 visitors in a decade, and now hosting over 50,000 guests in 2023. Renowned musicians like Gurukiran, Benny Dayal have graced the stage previously at the festival. Numerous talented artists have brought art and music back to life at Acharya Habba and shone their creativity along with various cultural showcases by the students.

Events such as multicultural Ethnic Day celebrations, Canvas Painting, Face Painting, Clay Modelling, Western and Classical Singing and Dance showcases, Faculty Talent Events, Cooking Events, Habbathon, Treasure Hunt, Coding Competitions, Cosplaying, Open Mic Events for Poetry Recitals and Comic Stand-Up Acts, are few of the events that make up Acharya Habba. Various Sports Leagues in Basketball, Volleyball, Kabaddi, and Acharya Premier League (Cricket) keep you on the edge of your seat.
