F A Q

Why do we have register ? - Registration is important for us to authorise that you are a student and can be allowed inside the premises.
How do I register for Habba 2024 ? - Click on the Events Registration button on homepage to register.
When will the Habba 2024 span? - From 20th April to 11th May, 2024.
Entry Procces ? - Acharya College physical ID! is mandatory. For Non-Acharyans bring a govt. issued ID Card.
Whom to contact in case of emergency ? - Contact to HABBA'24 core Team or mail us @ acharyahabba@acharya.ac.in
Why shall i give my information in registration ? - All the details asked are relevant and are used to keep you updated about events at HABBA'24. All the information is kept safe within the Acharya HABBA Database.

Acharya Habba, launched in 2005, is an annual college fest known for its diverse social and cultural experiences, featuring talent displays, music, sports, and collaborative events. With over 50 inter-collegiate competitions, it's one of Bangalore's top festivals, attracting students from various colleges. Over the years, it has expanded its size and reputation, drawing top performers and renowned figures from the music industry to its expansive campus.
