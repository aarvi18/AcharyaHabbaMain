About Habba

Acharya Habba is a Kaleidoscope of talent, expressed through a spectrum of performances which involves music, dance, drama, sports, technological prodigies with a tinge of competitive air and lots of cheer, diligence and ecstacy all around us. The fest harbours silver, gold or platinum sponsor titles with perks being provided accordingly, along with onlookers standing steadfast with awe and admiration at the splendid sight before them.

With events such as Battle of Bands, Fashion Show, and Choreo Night, each having unique themes, Acharya Habba glues all of its audience in their spots, making sure they never want it to end! It's that extraordinary time of the year where people from all over the country come to celebrate and create memories that last a lifetime. Drawing in a crowd of over 25000 students, more than half of them being in-house, it's the perfect time and place to meet new people and be alive in every sense of the word.

Cultural Mosaic Habba 2024

Welcome to the most anticipated event of the year Acharya Habba'24. Acharya Habba is the ultimate celebration of cultural diversity that brings together students from different parts of the world to celebrate and showcase their Unique culture in any form, be it dance, drama, music, art or even food!. With the theme of multiculturalism at its core, Acharya Habba provides a platform for students to express themselves and share their cultural heritage in a festive and welcoming atmosphere.

The festival is a melting pot of cultures with students performing traditional dances, music, and theater acts from various parts of the globe. As you step onto the vibrant campus, you'll be transported to a kaleidoscope of colors and sounds, each one representing a unique culture. The beats of the African Drums mingle with the lilting melodies of Indian Classical music, where Hip-hop meets Kathak, Raps form a jugalbandi with Sargams and everything comes together in a beautiful fusion to give you an experience like no other!

The Acharya Habba is more than just a celebration of cultures; it's a celebration of human diversity and the power of unity. Students of all backgrounds come together, forging new friendships, and learning from each other, as they embrace the rich tapestry of the world's many cultures.

Do's

Plan your day accordingly: Be on time and support your participating friends in their respective events have a look at the schedule and plan accordingly.

Dress to chill: The weather is very kind and its May, hence wear comfortable clothes & stay hydrated.

Entry and Exit: Entry is only through Gate no. 3 and Exit through Gate no. 1 & 2. Kindly co-operate with security and Habba crew & NCC Cadets.

Identify Check: Security personnel & Habba crew & NCC Cadets will be deployed across acharya campus, Please make sure to carry your physical ID cards of students (Driving License/ Aadhar card: For Outsiders).

Dont's

Not allowed: Vanity bags, Cosmetics, Keychains with sharp ends, Sharp objects or weapons (e.g.Nail cutter), Pepper sprays, Bangles or Kadas, Carrying liquid or edibles, including water bottles.

Dont be cheeky: Any kind of suspicious activites/ peace distruptions/ illegal activities will result in immediate suspension from the campus and the individual will be subjected to required actions.

We're watching you: Police, security personnel & habba crew & NCC Cadets will be deployed across the campus & have the right to detain any individual involved in any kind of activity that leads to disruption of peace within the venue.

Be Responsible: Management is not responsible for any kind of loss. We DO NOT provide any cloak room facility.
