About Habba

Acharya Habba is a vibrant showcase of various talents, including music, dance, drama, sports, and technology, accompanied by a spirit of friendly competition and abundant enthusiasm. The event attracts sponsors of varying levels, with perks provided accordingly, while spectators are left in awe and admiration at the impressive performances on display.

With events such as Battle of Bands, Fashion Show, and Choreo Night, each having unique themes, Acharya Habba glues all of its audience in their spots, making sure they never want it to end! It's that extraordinary time of the year where people from all over the country come to celebrate and create memories that last a lifetime. Drawing in a crowd of over 25000 students, more than half of them being in-house, it presents an ideal opportunity to socialize and fully immerse oneself in the experience.

Roar for the Forest - Protecting Tigers Den

Roar for the Forest: protecting Tiger's home" as this year's Acharya habba theme emphasizes the urgent need for conservation efforts to protect both forests and the iconic tiger species. The theme aims to raise awareness about the interconnectedness between forest ecosystems and the survival of tigers, highlighting the importance of preserving habitats and biodiversity. Through various activities, events, and educational initiatives, participants can learn about the threats facing forests and tigers, as well as the role they can play in conservation efforts. This theme serves as a call to action, inspiring individuals to take steps towards safeguarding our natural heritage for future generations and ensuring the continued existence of these majestic creatures in the wild.

Do's

Plan your day accordingly: Be on time and support your participating friends in their respective events have a look at the schedule and plan accordingly.

Dress to chill: The weather is very kind and its May, hence wear comfortable clothes & stay hydrated.

Entry and Exit: Entry is only through Gate no. 3 and Exit through Gate no. 1 & 2. Kindly co-operate with security and Habba crew & NCC Cadets.

Identify Check: Security personnel & Habba crew & NCC Cadets will be deployed across acharya campus, Please make sure to carry your physical ID cards of students (Driving License/ Aadhar card: For Outsiders).

Dont's

Not allowed: Vanity bags, Cosmetics, Keychains with sharp ends, Sharp objects or weapons (e.g.Nail cutter), Pepper sprays, Bangles or Kadas, Carrying liquid or edibles, including water bottles.

Dont be cheeky: Any kind of suspicious activites/ peace distruptions/ illegal activities will result in immediate suspension from the campus and the individual will be subjected to required actions.

We're watching you: Police, security personnel & habba crew & NCC Cadets will be deployed across the campus & have the right to detain any individual involved in any kind of activity that leads to disruption of peace within the venue.

Be Responsible: Management is not responsible for any kind of loss. We DO NOT provide any cloak room facility.

Acharya Habba, launched in 2005, is an annual college fest known for its diverse social and cultural experiences, featuring talent displays, music, sports, and collaborative events. With over 50 inter-collegiate competitions, it's one of Bangalore's top festivals, attracting students from various colleges. Over the years, it has expanded its size and reputation, drawing top performers and renowned figures from the music industry to its expansive campus.
